Cupcake Recipe

    Ingredients

  • 100g of self-raising flour
  • 100g of caster sugar
  • 100g of softened butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract

Method

Step 1: Pre-heat oven to 180C. Whisk 100g of softened butter with 100g of caster sugar until pale and fluffy. Then whisk two eggs.

Step 2: Add 1 tsp of vanilla ecxtract into the batter. Sift 100g of self-raising flour and whisk until the mixture is combined and smooth.

Step 3: Pour the mixture evenly into 12 cupcake cases. Bake in the oven for 12-15 minutes until the cupcakes are golden brown.
